TODO Karambeli - Sports

District 32 3A2 has adopted a village in Panvel Taluka of Raigad district called KARAMBELI. Since itâ€™s adoption around 3 yrs back, District has taken many developmental activities in the village. Village is situated on a hillock below the Sahyadri range of mountains. 70 families reside there with a population of around 365. The Adivasis residing here are mainly dependent on farming. Those who do not have their own land work as farm labourers. There is a ZP School in the village which provides education upto 7th grade. Around 65 students are studying in this school in different grades. There are 3 class rooms shared by all. Classroom verandas are also used as an independent classroom.
